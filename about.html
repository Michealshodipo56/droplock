HOW IT WORKS

INSTEAD OF LOGGING INTO A COMPANY'S SYSTEM, DROPLOCK LETS YOU CREATE YOUR OWN PRIVATE SPACES ON THE FLY. IF YOU CLOSE A ROOM AND FORGET THE PASSWORD, NO ONE CAN EVER OPEN IT AGAIN.

THE TERMINAL

THE HOMEPAGE

The homepage looks like an old-school computer terminal. This is your main gateway into DropLock. You won't find a "Sign Up" button here. Instead, you just type in any name you want to create a space.

HOW TO ENTER
A LOCKER

To get started, follow these simple steps right from the Terminal homepage:

  • 01 LOCKER ID: Type any word you want into the top box (like "MySecretProject"). Think of this as the name of your room.
  • 02 PASSWORD: Type a strong password into the bottom box. Since we do not save your password, you must remember it!
  • 03 ACCESS: Click the card at the bottom that flashes "SYSTEM UNLOCKED". If the room doesn't exist, it creates it instantly. If it does exist, it opens!
LOCKER

INSIDE YOUR
PRIVATE LOCKER

Once you unlock your space, you enter your Private Locker. This is your personal dashboard for working, saving files, and writing notes safely.

  • A THE TEXT EDITOR: The large typing area in the middle is where you can write notes, paste code, or type essays. It automatically encrypts your words.
  • B THE FILE VAULT: Look at the bottom area that says "DRAG & DROP ASSETS". You can drag pictures, videos, or documents here to save them to your locker forever.
  • C THE SAVE BUTTON: At the top of your screen, there is a "SAVE" button. Always click this before you leave to make sure your latest notes and files are locked away!

LOCKER TOOLS

Besides saving and writing, the Locker has a few extra tools to protect you:

ACCOUNT RECOVERY THEME TOGGLE RELOAD SYNC

Theme Toggle: At the bottom of the sidebar inside your locker, there is a "TOGGLE THEME" button. Click it to switch between Dark and Light mode to suit your working environment.

Account Recovery: We can't reset passwords, but if you go to your Locker settings, you can attach an email address to get a secret code in case you ever forget your password.

THE TWO FILE TRANSFER METHODS

DropLock includes an entirely separate area just for sending files to other people. Click "TRANSFER FILES" at the top of any page to get started. Here is how both methods work:

METHOD 01 : DIRECT P2P TRANSFER

This method connects your computer directly to your friend's computer. It is the fastest and safest because the file never touches our servers.

  • 1 OPEN RADAR: Both you and your friend must go to the Transfer page and make sure "DIRECT P2P" is selected on the left menu.
  • 2 SCAN: Click the large "SCAN" button in the center. Your radar will spin, and you will see your friend's computer pop up.
  • 3 CONNECT: Click on your friend's name. A box will open asking you to accept the connection.
  • 4 SEND: Drag and drop your files. It beams directly to them!
METHOD 02 : SECURE 6-DIGIT CODE

Use this method if your friend is not online right now. You upload the file to our temporary RAM servers, and they download it later.

  • 1 SELECT ENCRYPTED: On the Transfer page, click the "ENCRYPTED" option on the left menu sidebar.
  • 2 UPLOAD FILE: A box will appear. Drag your file into the box to start uploading.
  • 3 GET THE CODE: The system will give you a bright cyan 6-digit code (e.g., 842-198). Send this code to your friend in a text message.
  • 4 THEY DOWNLOAD: Your friend opens DropLock, selects "ENCRYPTED", types in that 6-digit code, and downloads the file. Wait—it gets better: the file is automatically deleted forever after 5 minutes!
