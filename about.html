WHAT IS DROPLOCK?

DropLock is a high-security, anonymous file exchange platform designed for users who prioritize privacy above everything else. We built DropLock on the principle that privacy is a human right, not a feature.

Unlike traditional cloud storage, DropLock does not want to know who you are. We don't ask for emails, we don't track IPs for marketing, and we certainly don't store your data in a way that we—or anyone else—can read it.

HOW IT WORKS

01. THE LOCKER

Your own private vault. Create a locker by simply entering a name and a strong password. This locker is your persistent home for notes and sensitive files. Everything inside is encrypted with your password before it leaves your browser.

02. DIRECT TRANSFER

Need to send a file to someone right now? Direct Transfer creates an ephemeral link between two devices. Once the receiver downloads the file, the link is destroyed. No traces, no history.

03. ENCRYPTED CODE TRANSFER

Generate a 6-digit secure code, upload your files, and share the code. The receiver enters the code to pull the files. These transfers are held in system RAM and are automatically deleted after 30 minutes. They never touch a hard drive.

THE TECHNOLOGY

DropLock is powered by a high-performance Go backend and a Zero-Knowledge frontend architecture.

  • AES-256-GCM: Military-grade encryption for all file payloads.
  • Argon2id: State-of-the-art key derivation to protect your Locker passwords from brute-force attacks.
  • Memory-Only Storage: Transfers are kept in RAM, ensuring that even a physical seizure of the server would yield zero usable data.
  • Client-Side Processing: We offload as much as possible to your browser, so you stay in control of your encryption keys.
