PRIVACY_POLICY

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2026

01. DATA COLLECTION

DropLock does not collect any personal information. We do not require accounts, registration, or contact details to use our core services. Your usage remains anonymous by default.

  • No IP Logging: We do not store or track your IP address.
  • No Cookies: We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

02. DATA PROTECTION

All data uploaded to DropLock is treated as hostile. This means we assume someone is always trying to intercept it, and we protect it accordingly.

ZERO-KNOWLEDGE ENCRYPTION

Every file and note you save in a Locker is encrypted on your device using your password. Since we never receive your password in plaintext, it is mathematically impossible for us to decrypt or view your data.

03. DATA RETENTION

We believe data that doesn't exist cannot be stolen. Our retention policies are designed to minimize the storage of sensitive information.

Service Retention Period Storage Method
Locker Data Indefinite (Until manually deleted) Encrypted Disk Storage
Direct Transfer Instant (Deleted after download) System RAM
Encrypted Transfer 30 Minutes (Automatic purge) System RAM

04. THIRD-PARTIES

DropLock is independent. We do not share data with third-party advertisers, government agencies, or data brokers. Our infrastructure is self-contained and focused on a single mission: Anonymity.

Transparency is the foundation of trust. For technical details on our encryption protocols, please visit our About Page.
