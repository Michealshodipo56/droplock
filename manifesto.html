PRIVACY

This Privacy Policy explains what information DropLock handles, how that information is used, and the choices you have when using the website and its file transfer and locker features.

INFORMATION_COLLECTION

DropLock is designed to be usable without creating a traditional account. You can use the service with a locker name and password instead of a profile, username, or public identity.

Depending on how you use the service, we may handle the following information:

  • Locker names, password hashes, notes, and files stored in a locker.
  • Files sent through direct transfer or encrypted code transfer.
  • Optional recovery email information if you choose to add it to a locker.
  • Basic technical request data that may be created by the server during operation and troubleshooting.

USAGE_AND_SHARING

We use information only to operate the service you request, including creating lockers, storing notes and files, processing transfers, helping you access your locker, and maintaining site reliability and security.

We do not use the service to build advertising profiles, and we do not require a marketing account to use core features.

SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to run the service, comply with legal obligations, protect the website, or enforce our terms and security controls.

STORAGE_AND_SECURITY

Locker content is stored until you delete it or remove the locker. Temporary transfer data is kept only for the time needed to complete the transfer flow and is automatically cleaned up after a short period.

At the time of this policy, encrypted code transfers are removed automatically after about 5 minutes. Direct transfer session data is also temporary and is not intended for long-term storage.

SECURITY AND ANALYTICS

We use technical and operational measures intended to protect stored and transferred data. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should avoid uploading information unless you are comfortable with the risks of using an internet-based service.

DropLock is not built around third-party advertising or account tracking. We do not describe the service as depending on ad-tech profiling to function.

YOUR_CHOICES

You can choose what information you upload, whether to add a recovery email, and whether to keep or delete content in your locker. If you no longer want data stored in a locker, you can remove it from the service.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page so you can see when the latest version became effective.
